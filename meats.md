# Meat Cooking Temperatures

## Beef & Lamb

| Internal Temp | Doneness   | Meats                        |
|---------------|------------|------------------------------|
| 45°C / 113°F  | Rare       | Beef                         |
| 50°C / 122°F  | Medium-rare| Beef                         |
| 60°C / 140°F  | Medium     | Beef, lamb, venison, duck    |
| 72°C / 162°F  | Well done  | Beef, lamb                   |

## Pork

| Internal Temp | Doneness   | Notes                        |
|---------------|------------|------------------------------|
| 63°C / 145°F  | Medium     | Rest 3 min before serving    |
| 71°C / 160°F  | Well done  | Ground pork                  |

## Poultry (Chicken & Turkey)

| Internal Temp | Doneness   | Notes                           |
|---------------|------------|---------------------------------|
| 74°C / 165°F  | Safe/done  | All cuts, whole bird, ground    |

## Fish & Seafood

| Internal Temp | Doneness   | Notes                           |
|---------------|------------|---------------------------------|
| 52°C / 125°F  | Medium-rare| Salmon, tuna steaks             |
| 57°C / 135°F  | Medium     | Most fish fillets               |
| 63°C / 145°F  | Well done  | All fish (USDA safe minimum)    |
| 74°C / 165°F  | Safe/done  | Shrimp, lobster, crab, scallops |

## Quick Reference

```
45°C (113°F) – Rare        – Beef
50°C (122°F) – Medium-rare – Beef
60°C (140°F) – Medium      – Beef, lamb, venison, duck
63°C (145°F) – Safe        – Pork, whole fish
72°C (162°F) – Well done   – Beef, lamb, pork (ground)
74°C (165°F) – Safe/done   – Poultry (all), seafood
```
